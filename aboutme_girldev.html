Short Bio

I am originally from a tiny ski resort town in Alaska, and spent the 8 years or so after highschool following the snow around the pacific northwest. I was a sponsored snowboarder competing in slopestyle and other events. Taking university courses part-time eventually landed me with a B.Sc. in Physics, and one summer I even got to do a research project at the NASA Jet Propulsion Lab. This got me interested in cosmology, which I decided to pursue in graduate school. My love of the universe, combined with my love for the mountains and snow, is what brought me to beautiful Vancouver. I plan to finish my PhD at UBC sometime next winter.
